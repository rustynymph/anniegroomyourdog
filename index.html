Grooming Services

Bath & Basics Groom

The "Bath & Basics" Groom is for dogs that are hairless or have very short coats that just need a bath and have basic grooming needs that require no hair trimming and very limited-to-no brushing.

Includes:

  • Bath with shampoo and conditioning
  • Blow dry & deshed or light brush out
  • Nails, ear cleaning, teeth cleaning
  • Trimming only incudes paw pads and sanitary area (if necessary)
  • Prices start at:
  • $60 for very short coats, smooth-coats, or hairless dogs (e.g. chihuahuah)
  • $70 for medium coats, double-coats (e.g. corgi, mini aussie, mini schnauzer)
  • $80 for long coats and/or curly coats (e.g. poodle and poodle-mixes, bichon frise, drop-coat breeds with long hair)

Tidy Up Groom

The "Tidy Up" Groom is for dogs with medium and longer coats that don't need a full haircut at the moment, but need a good bath, blowout, and light trimup in a few areas.

Includes:

  • Bath with shampoo and conditioning
  • Blow dry, brush out, & deshed
  • Nails, ear cleaning, teeth cleaning
  • Trimming includes head, face and eyes, tail, feet, paw pads, and sanitary area. No overall body or leg trimming
  • Prices start at:
  • $85 for medium coats, double-coats (e.g. mini aussie, mini schnauzer, maltese)
  • $100 for long coats and/or curly coats (e.g. poodle and poodle-mixes, bichon frise, drop-coat breeds with long hair)

More Info

Full Groom

The "Full" Groom is for dogs that need a full clipper or handscissored haircut or outline perimter trim.

Includes:

  • Bath with shampoo and conditioning
  • Blow dry, brush out, & deshed
  • Nails, ear cleaning, teeth cleaning
  • Full haircut
  • Prices start at:
  • $100 for medium coats, double-coats (e.g. mini aussie, mini schnauzer, maltese)
  • $120 for long coats and/or curly coats (e.g. poodle and poodle-mixes, bichon frise, drop-coat breeds with long hair)
