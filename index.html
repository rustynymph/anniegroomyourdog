Services

Bath & Basics Groom

The "Bath & Basics" Groom is for dogs that are hairless or have very short coats that just need a bath and require no hair trimming and very limited-to-no brushing (e.g. Frenchies, Smooth-coated Daschunds, Chihuahuahs).

Includes:

  • Bath with shampoo and conditioning
  • Blow dry & deshed or light brush out
  • Nails, ear cleaning, teeth cleaning
  • Trimming only incudes paw pads and sanitary area (if necessary)

$65-$85

Tidy Up Groom

The "Tidy Up" Groom is for dogs with medium-to-longer coats that don't need a full haircut at the moment, but need a good bath, blowout, and light trimup in a few areas. Some might consider this an "in-between" haircut or a "maintenance" groom (e.g. a Mini Goldendoodle that just needs their feet and eyes trimmed, a Mini Aussie that just needs a deshed).

Includes:

  • Bath with shampoo and conditioning
  • Blow dry, brush out, & deshed
  • Nails, ear cleaning, teeth cleaning
  • Trimming includes head, face and eyes, tail, feet, paw pads, and sanitary area. No overall body or leg trimming

Starting at $85

Full Groom

The "Full" Groom is for dogs that need a full clipper or handscissored haircut or outline perimter trim.

Includes:

  • Bath with shampoo and conditioning
  • Blow dry, brush out, & deshed
  • Nails, ear cleaning, teeth cleaning
  • Full haircut

Starting at $100

About Me

Hi there! My name is Annie. I was born and raised in NY, but have been a proud Coloradan since 2012. I always dreamed of a career where I could channel my creative and artistic side while doing something impactful and caring for others. I've always loved animals since I was a kid, and am currently a mom to a grumpy senior cat and a wild little puppy. When I realized that a career in pet grooming aligned with those dreams, I quit my former career to start an apprenticeship, and never looked back.

Since then I have fallen in love with this career and have been humbled by the trust and appreciation I've received from clients. Let's be honest, grooming can be stressful and a bit frightening for many pets, which is why I take my time and offer one-one-one services in your home. It is important to me to earn the trust of sensitive dogs by learning to work with their needs, even if it means working a little slower. I promise to show your dog the compassion and respect they deserve.

It's important to me to never stop educating myself, which is why I continue to attend seminars, conferences, and workshops to stay up to date with the latest techniques. I enjoy learning new creative ways of grooming such as Asian Fusion and hair coloring, as well as timeless techniques and breed standards.

Your pet's safety and wellbeing are very important to me, which I why I am K-9 First Aid and CPR Certified from a hands-on course led by a Colorado First Responder, as well as Disease and Sanitation Certified from the American Kennel Club. I am also licensed through PACFA and insured through Pet Care Insurance. If you think we would be a good match to work together, please reach out!
